Checklist For Final Project

  • All your images should be RGB, not CMYK images
  • All image formats should be .jpg, .png, or .gif. No .psd images
  • Make sure you have no hidden Mac files
  • For faster downloads, your images should not be greater than 70KB
  • Your entire site should be less than 8MB
  • Make sure all your links work
  • Make sure you have </title> in the head section
  • Your final project worksheet should be filled out with all .html filenames and one .css name
  • Your site should work in at least two of the major browsers: Google Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari and Opera
  • Be sure to validate your site with a validator, such as W3C Markup Validator
