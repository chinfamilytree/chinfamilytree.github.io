Homework 7 - Rollover and Image Map

Copy all the files from the hw6 folder to the hw7 folder.

Copy the following images from "Assets" to the hw7 folder:

   ophelia-r.jpg
   spring-r.jpg
   spring.jpg
   waterhouse.jpg

Open waterhouse.jpg in Photoshop to find the coordinates for the
three hotspots:

  • a circle for his left eye
  • a rectangle on his forehead
  • a polygon for his ear

In Photoshop... Window/Info
In Info make sure the cross in the X: Y: section shows pixels.

For the circle you need to specify the X, Y coordinates of the center
of the circle and the Radius...

  • Move your mouse to the center of his left eye, note the X and Y coordinates, such as 93, 78.
  • To get the radius for the circle, move your mouse straight to the right and note the
