亠 Radical 8

8 亠 2 亠 hẽo tóu Kangxi radical 8, is a component cosisting of a dot,丶, and a horizontal stroke 一 meaning 'lid' or 'cover'. It is called 头[頭] hẽo tóu, but more commonly referred to as 文字头[文字頭] mũn-dù-hẽo wénzìtóu.
<台> The component, 亠, is commonly called ēm-ēm-vãng-vâk 点点横画[點點橫畫].
